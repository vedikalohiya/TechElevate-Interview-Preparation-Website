Time and Work Questions

1. A can complete a work in 12 days and B can complete it in 18 days. How long will they take to complete the work together?

They will complete the work together in 7.2 days. (1/12 + 1/18 = 5/36; Total work = 36/5 = 7.2 days).

2. A can complete a task in 15 hours, B in 25 hours, and C in 30 hours. How long will they take to complete the task if all work together?

They will complete the task in 8 hours. (1/15 + 1/25 + 1/30 = 31/150; Total work = 150/31 = 8 hours).

3. A and B can finish a job in 8 days and 12 days respectively. How long will they take to complete the job together?

A and B together will complete the work in 4.8 days. (1/8 + 1/12 = 5/24; Total work = 24/5 = 4.8 days).

4. A can do a piece of work in 10 days. If A and B can finish the work together in 6 days, how long will B take to do the work alone?

B will take 15 days to complete the work alone. (1/10 + 1/b = 1/6; b = 15).

5. If A and B together can complete a work in 8 days, and A alone can complete the work in 12 days, how long will B take to complete the work alone?

B will take 24 days. (1/8 - 1/12 = 1/24).

6. A can do a job in 20 days, B in 30 days. How long will they take to complete the job together?

A and B together will complete the job in 12 days. (1/20 + 1/30 = 5/60; Total work = 60/5 = 12 days).

7. A and B can finish a work in 16 days and 24 days respectively. How long will they take to complete the work together?

A and B together will complete the work in 9.6 days. (1/16 + 1/24 = 5/48; Total work = 48/5 = 9.6 days).

8. A can do a piece of work in 5 days. B can do the same work in 8 days. How long will they take to finish the work together?

A and B together will finish the work in 3.14 days. (1/5 + 1/8 = 13/40; Total work = 40/13 ≈ 3.14 days).

9. A can complete a work in 12 days. B can complete the same work in 15 days. How long will they take to complete the work if they work together for 4 days?

They will complete 7/10 of the work. (1/12 + 1/15 = 3/20; In 4 days, they complete 12/20 = 7/10 of the work).

10. A can do a job in 25 days. How long will it take for A to complete 3/5 of the work?

A will complete 3/5 of the work in 15 days. (25 days for full work, so 3/5 of it = 15 days).

11. A can complete a task in 6 days, B in 8 days. How long will they take to complete the task together?

A and B together will complete the work in 3.43 days. (1/6 + 1/8 = 7/24; Total work = 24/7 ≈ 3.43 days).

12. A and B together can do a piece of work in 12 days, and B can do it alone in 18 days. How long will A take to do the work alone?

A will take 36 days to complete the work alone. (1/12 - 1/18 = 1/36).

13. A can complete a task in 20 days, B can do the same in 30 days. How long will they take to finish the task if A works alone for 10 days and B works alone for 5 days?

The task will be completed in 12.5 days. A completes 10/20 in 10 days and B completes 5/30 in 5 days, so total = 15/20 of work, requiring 12.5 days for the remaining work.

14. A can finish a task in 12 days and B in 16 days. If they work together, how long will it take to finish the task?

A and B will finish the work together in 6.86 days. (1/12 + 1/16 = 7/48; Total work = 48/7 ≈ 6.86 days).

16. If A and B can finish a job in 9 days, and A works alone for 5 days, how long will it take B to finish the remaining work?

B will complete the remaining work in 7.5 days. (1/9 = 1/5 + 1/x; x ≈ 7.5).

17. A can complete a task in 10 days and B in 15 days. How long will they take to finish the work together?
