Quantitative Aptitude Questions

1. If the salary is $5000, how much does he pay in rent?

Answer: He pays $1500 in rent.

2. What is 25% of 2400?

Answer: 600.

3. A shopkeeper sells a product for $500, which costs him $400. What is his profit percentage?

Answer: Profit = Selling Price - Cost Price = 500 - 400 = 100. Profit Percentage = (Profit / Cost Price) × 100 = (100 / 400) × 100 = 25%.

4. If a car travels 60 miles in 1 hour, how much distance will it travel in 5 hours?

Answer: Distance = Speed × Time = 60 × 5 = 300 miles.

5. The cost of 5 pens and 7 pencils is $45. If the cost of a pen is $5, what is the cost of a pencil?

Answer: Let the cost of a pencil be 'x'. The equation becomes 5(5) + 7x = 45. Simplifying: 25 + 7x = 45, so 7x = 20, hence x = 20 / 7 = $2.86 approximately.

6. A sum of $1500 is invested at 8% annual interest. How much interest will be earned in 2 years?

Answer: Interest = Principal × Rate × Time / 100 = 1500 × 8 × 2 / 100 = $240.

7. A train runs at 72 km/h. How much time will it take to cover a distance of 180 km?

Answer: Time = Distance / Speed = 180 / 72 = 2.5 hours.

8. What is the area of a triangle with base 10 cm and height 12 cm?

Answer: Area of triangle = (Base × Height) / 2 = (10 × 12) / 2 = 60 cm².

9. If a person spends 30% of his income on rent, and his monthly income is $3000, how much does he spend on rent?

Answer: Rent spent = 30% of 3000 = (30/100) × 3000 = $900.

10. A man can complete a task in 12 hours. How much time will it take him to finish the task if he works at twice the speed?

Answer: If the speed is doubled, time will be halved. Time taken = 12 / 2 = 6 hours.

11. The ratio of the ages of two brothers is 4:5. If the elder brother is 25 years old, what is the age of the younger brother?

Answer: Let the age of the younger brother be x. According to the ratio, 4x = 25 × 4/5, hence x = 20 years.

12. A car depreciates in value by 10% per year. If it is worth $15000 now, what will be its value after 2 years?

Answer: After 1st year: 15000 × (1 - 0.10) = $13500. After 2nd year: 13500 × (1 - 0.10) = $12150.

13. The length of a rectangle is 20 cm more than its breadth. If the perimeter of the rectangle is 160 cm, find its dimensions.

Answer: Let the breadth be x cm. Length = x + 20 cm. Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Breadth) = 160 cm. Solving, x = 30 cm, Length = 50 cm.

14. The price of a book is increased by 25%. If the original price was $120, what is the new price?

Answer: New price = 120 × (1 + 0.25) = $150.

15. A person invested $5000 at 10% compound interest. How much interest will he earn in 2 years?

Answer: Compound Interest = 5000 × (1 + 0.10)² - 5000 = $1050.

16. A bag contains 3 red, 5 blue, and 2 green balls. If one ball is drawn at random, what is the probability that it is red?

Answer: Probability = (Number of red balls) / (Total number of balls) = 3/10.

17. If 15% of a number is 75, what is the number?

Answer: Let the number be x. 15% of x = 75, so x = 75 / 0.15 = 500.

18. A person can walk 5 km in 1 hour. How far can he walk in 5 hours and 30 minutes?

Answer: Distance = 5 × 5.5 = 27.5 km.

19. The sum of the ages of a mother and her daughter is 50 years. The mother is four times as old as the daughter. Find their ages.

Answer: Let the daughter's age be x. The mother's age = 4x. Hence, x + 4x = 50, so x = 10 years. Mother’s age = 40 years.

20. A man completes a task in 8 hours. If he works 1.5 times as fast, how much time will he take to finish the same task?

Answer: If the speed increases, the time taken decreases. Time = 8 / 1.5 = 5.33 hours.
