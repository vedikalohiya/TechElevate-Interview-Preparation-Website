Data Interpretation Questions

1. A survey shows 40% of people prefer A, 30% prefer B, and 30% prefer C. What is the probability of picking someone who prefers A?

Answer: 0.40 or 40%

2. If the average of five numbers is 20, what is the sum of the numbers?

Answer: 100

3. The average of 10 numbers is 15. If one number is removed, the average becomes 14. What is the removed number?

Answer: 25

4. A class has 30 students. 18 of them passed in Mathematics, 20 passed in Science, and 15 passed in both subjects. How many students failed in both subjects?

Answer: 5

5. In a company, 60% of employees are male and 40% are female. If 30% of the males and 50% of the females work in the marketing department, what percentage of employees work in marketing?

Answer: 42%

6. A student scored 75%, 85%, and 90% in three subjects. What is the average percentage?

Answer: 83.33%

7. The average of five numbers is 50. If one number is 70, what is the average of the remaining numbers?

Answer: 40

8. A survey shows that 60% of people like chocolate, 40% like vanilla, and 10% like both. What is the probability that a randomly chosen person likes either chocolate or vanilla?

Answer: 0.90 or 90%

9. If the price of a product increases by 20%, what is the new price if the original price was 500?

Answer: 600

10. A shopkeeper offers a 25% discount on an item. If the item’s original price is 400, what is the selling price?

Answer: 300

11. A train travels 120 km in 2 hours. What is the speed of the train?

Answer: 60 km/h

12. A person invests $1000 at an annual interest rate of 5%. What is the interest after 2 years?

Answer: $100

13. The sum of the ages of a father and son is 50 years. If the father is four times as old as the son, how old are they?

Answer: Father is 40 years old, Son is 10 years old.

14. If the median of five numbers is 10, and the numbers are in increasing order, what is the third number?

Answer: 10

15. A car travels 200 km in 4 hours. What is its average speed?

Answer: 50 km/h

16. If the ratio of the ages of two friends is 5:6, and the sum of their ages is 55, what are their ages?

Answer: 25 years and 30 years.

17. If a number is increased by 25%, the new number becomes 125. What is the original number?

Answer: 100

18. What is the average of 12, 15, 18, 22, and 25?

Answer: 18.4

19. A company’s revenue for the last 5 years has been 100, 120, 150, 130, and 160 million. What is the average revenue?

Answer: 132 million

20. A person saves 30% of his income. If his income is $2000, how much does he save?

Answer: $600
