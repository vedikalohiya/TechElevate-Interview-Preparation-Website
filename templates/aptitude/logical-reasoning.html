Logical Reasoning Questions

1. If all cats are animals, and some animals are dogs, can we conclude that some cats are dogs?

No, we cannot conclude that some cats are dogs. The statements do not provide enough information.

2. If all roses are flowers, and all flowers are plants, can we conclude that all roses are plants?

Yes, all roses are plants because all roses are flowers, and all flowers are plants.

3. If A is taller than B, and B is taller than C, who is the shortest?

C is the shortest because A is taller than B, and B is taller than C.

4. If two pencils cost $1, how much will five pencils cost?

Five pencils will cost $2.50 because two pencils cost $1, so one pencil costs $0.50.

5. A man is 4 times as old as his son. In 20 years, the man will be twice as old as his son. How old are they now?

The man is 40 years old, and the son is 10 years old. (Let the son's age be x, then the man's age is 4x. In 20 years, the man's age will be 4x+20, and the son's age will be x+20. We set the equation 4x+20 = 2(x+20) and solve for x).

6. If the day after tomorrow is two days before Friday, what day is it today?

Today is Wednesday. The day after tomorrow is Thursday, which is two days before Friday.

7. If 3 cats catch 3 mice in 3 minutes, how many mice will 6 cats catch in 6 minutes?

6 cats will catch 12 mice in 6 minutes because 1 cat catches 1 mouse in 3 minutes, so in 6 minutes, it will catch 2 mice. Thus, 6 cats will catch 12 mice.

8. If 5 workers can complete a task in 10 days, how many days will 10 workers take to complete the same task?

10 workers will take 5 days to complete the task. The number of days taken is inversely proportional to the number of workers.

9. If it takes 3 minutes to boil an egg, how many minutes will it take to boil 5 eggs?

It will take 3 minutes to boil 5 eggs, assuming all eggs are boiled at the same time.

10. A clock shows the time as 3:15. What angle is formed between the hour and minute hands?

The angle between the hour and minute hands is 7.5 degrees.

12. How many months have 28 days?

All 12 months have at least 28 days.

13. If you have a 10-liter tank, and you need to fill it with water, how many times do you need to fill the tank if you can only fill 2 liters at a time?

You need to fill the tank 5 times, as each time you can fill 2 liters.

15. If you travel at 60 miles per hour, how long will it take to travel 120 miles?

16. If the sum of two numbers is 20 and their difference is 4, what are the two numbers?

The two numbers are 12 and 8 (x + y = 20 and x - y = 4, solve the system of equations).

17. What comes next in the sequence: 1, 4, 9, 16, ...?

The next number is 25. The sequence consists of perfect squares (1^2, 2^2, 3^2, 4^2, etc.).

18. If 7 apples cost $14, how much will 5 apples cost?

5 apples will cost $10, as each apple costs $2.

19. How many sides does a regular hexagon have?

A regular hexagon has 6 sides.
